| S-DN | Day/Night Switch | 2000mm above FFL | All buildings (external circuits) |
| S-ISO30 | 30A Isolator Switch | 2000mm above FFL | All buildings (geyser/heavy loads) |
| S-ISO20 | 20A Isolator Switch | 2000mm above FFL | All buildings (AC units) |
| S-1L2W | 1-lever, 2-Way Switch | 1200mm above FFL | Community Hall, Guard Houses, Pool Block |
| S-MS | Master Switch | — | Community Hall only (controls hall lighting banks) |

**Extraction Notes:**
- Day/Night switches control external/security lighting circuits — always paired with flood lights or pole lights
- Isolator switches indicate dedicated circuits (AC, geyser, heavy equipment) — each requires separate breaker and cable run
- 2-Way switches appear in corridors/passages with multiple entry points
- Master switches in Community Hall control multiple lighting banks simultaneously

## 10. KEY OBSERVATIONS & WARNINGS FOR AFRIPLAN EXTRACTION

1. **TWO SEPARATE ELECTRICAL SUPPLIES** — The NewMark office has its own Eskom kiosk connection (separate from the Wedela complex DB-CR). The AI must detect this as two independent systems.

2. **WATTAGE CONFLICTS** — Community Hall SLD shows different wattage calculations than what fixture counts on layouts would suggest. SLD schedule should be preferred (consultant's final calculation).

3. **MASSIVE CABLE RUNS** — The 110m run of 95mm² cable from Kiosk to DB-CR is an extremely high-cost item. Cable run distances are explicitly marked on the Outside Lights drawing — the AI must extract these.

4. **POOL INFRASTRUCTURE** — 8 pool pumps, 9 heat pumps, 2 circulation pumps — all with dedicated DBs, VSDs, and heavy cable. This is likely 30-40% of the total project cost.

5. **IP RATINGS** — Wet areas (ablutions, pool, outdoor) use vapor proof and waterproof fittings. The AI must distinguish between standard and IP-rated items for accurate pricing.

6. **GEYSER COUNT** — 9 × 50L geysers in Ablution Block alone, each with 30A isolator. Plus geysers in other buildings. All require dedicated circuits.

7. **DIFFERENT LEGEND SETS** — The NewMark office drawings have a DIFFERENT legend set (includes data points, floor boxes, power skirting, cable tray) that doesn't appear in the Wedela buildings. The AI must handle building-specific legends.

8. **GUARD HOUSE DAY/NIGHT BYPASS** — The Large Guard House has a Day/Night switch with bypass arrangement (S1→S2 switching). This is more complex than a standard day/night switch and needs specific wiring.

9. **THREE DIFFERENT DRAWING FORMATS** — NewMark uses TJM-xxx numbering, Wedela uses WD-xxx numbering. Different title blocks, different clients. The AI must handle multi-project documents.

10. **SOLAR PANEL PROVISION** — NewMark building has underground sleeves provisioned for future solar cables (50mm × 3, 75mm × 4, 110mm × 2). These are civil/electrical hybrid items.
