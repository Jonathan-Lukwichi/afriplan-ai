## What Is AfriPlan?

AfriPlan Electrical is an AI-powered platform that automatically extracts quantities from electrical drawings and generates professional Bills of Quantities (BOQs) for tendering.

### How It Works


1. **Upload** your electrical drawings (PDF or images)
2. **AI extracts** all quantities automatically:
   - Distribution boards (DBs)
   - Circuits with breaker sizes
   - Light fittings (12 types)
   - Sockets & switches (8 types)
   - Cable runs and lengths
3. **Download** a professional Excel BOQ ready for tendering

| Category | Items Extracted |
|----------|-----------------|
